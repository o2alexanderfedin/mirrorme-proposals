## Executive Summary

Transform MirrorMe from a single San Francisco location into a national franchise brand with 50-100 locations across major U.S. metropolitan areas. The automated photostudio model is highly franchise-ready with proven unit economics, low labor requirements, and scalable technology.

**Market Validation**: The Korean model (Life Four Cuts) has 410+ franchise locations and is rapidly expanding to the U.S., validating massive demand for self-service portrait studios.

**Franchise Economics**: $110K-$165K initial investment, $306K-$428K annual revenue per location, 30-45% net margins for franchisees.

**Competitive Timing**: **URGENT** - Life Four Cuts is actively expanding in 18+ U.S. states via H Mart partnerships. MirrorMe must move quickly to establish brand presence before the Korean giant saturates the market.

**Franchise-Ready Requirements**:

#### Priority 1 - Standardized Technology Stack

**Central Editing Cloud**:

- **Challenge**: Current editing is location-specific; need centralized processing
- **Solution**: Build cloud-based editing service (all locations upload to central system)
- **Benefits**: Consistent quality, centralized updates, lower per-location costs
- **Technology**: AWS/GCP with GPU instances, S3 storage, automated processing queues
- **Estimated Cost**: $50K-$100K initial development + $2K-$5K/month cloud hosting
- **Timeline**: 3-6 months development

**Franchise Portal**:

- **Dashboard**: Franchisee revenue tracking, bookings, customer feedback
- **Inventory Management**: Equipment tracking, maintenance schedules
- **Support Ticketing**: Technical support requests to franchisor
- **Training Modules**: Video tutorials, SOPs, certification programs
- **Technology**: Web-based SaaS (React frontend, Node.js backend)
- **Estimated Cost**: $30K-$60K development
- **Timeline**: 2-4 months development

**Booking System Standardization**:

- **Multi-Location Support**: Central booking with location selection
- **Brand Consistency**: Identical UX across all locations
- **Integration**: Connect to franchise portal for analytics
- **Options**: Square (current), Acuity Scheduling, or custom-built
- **Estimated Cost**: $10K-$20K if staying with Square; $50K+ if custom
- **Timeline**: 1-2 months integration
