## Executive Summary

Transform MirrorMe's single-location consumer photostudio into a dual-revenue model by launching a B2B Corporate Headshot-as-a-Service program targeting San Francisco Bay Area companies. This addresses the client's core need: **occupy the studio from 9-5** with predictable, recurring revenue from corporate clients.

**Target Market**: 50,000+ Bay Area companies with 10+ employees needing professional headshots for LinkedIn, websites, and employee directories.

**Revenue Potential**: $240K-$480K additional annual revenue from corporate subscriptions (doubling current estimated revenue).

**Client Value Proposition**: 66-73% cost savings vs. traditional corporate photography, same-day delivery, and guaranteed consistency.
